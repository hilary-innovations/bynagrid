Privacy Policy

CryptoLex is built with privacy by design. We collect no personal data whatsoever.

1. Who We Are

CryptoLex is an independent word puzzle game. Bundle ID: com.cryptolex.puzzle.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We collect zero personal information.

  • No names, emails, or contact info
  • No location or GPS
  • No device or advertising IDs
  • No camera, microphone, or photos
  • No financial data
  • No cross-app tracking

3. Local Storage Only

Game progress (coins, stars, scores, settings) is stored only on your device via SharedPreferences.

🔒 Nothing is ever transmitted. We have no servers.

4. Internet Permission

Declared as a Flutter SDK standard requirement. Not used to send or receive any data. Full offline play supported.

5. Third-Party SDKs

None. No analytics, no ads, no crash reporters, no trackers of any kind.

6. Children (COPPA)

Safe for all ages. No data collected from anyone, including children under 13.

7. GDPR

No personal data collected — fully GDPR compliant by design.

8. Delete Your Data

Uninstall the app. All local data removed automatically.
